About

Hello! I'm a senior manager in technical writing who still writes — not because I have to, but because I want to.

This site, Docs and Direction, reflects my journey through the layered landscape of software documentation, team leadership, content strategy, and continuous learning. I believe the best leaders are those who never let go of the craft that got them there.

Over the years, I’ve worked on simplifying complex technologies, building documentation strategies, mentoring writers, and collaborating with product teams. Along the way, I’ve realized that direction isn’t just about strategy — it’s about clarity, empathy, and choosing the right words.

So whether you’re here for inspiration, practical advice, or just curious — welcome aboard!
